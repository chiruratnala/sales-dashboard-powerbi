Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard
====================================

Project Overview
----------------

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of supermarket sales data across three different branches over a three-month period. The goal of this dashboard is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights regarding revenue, profitability, and customer demographics.

Key Features & Insights
-----------------------

*   **Executive KPIs**: Tracks total **Revenue (320.15K)**, total **Profit (15.25K)**, and **Total Transactions (990)** at a glance.
    
*   **Regional Performance**: Compares profitability across the **Alex (34.1%)**, **Cairo (32.96%)**, and **Giza (32.94%)** branches.
    
*   **Product Line Analysis**: Visualizes sales distribution across categories such as **Food and Beverages**, **Fashion**, and **Electronic Accessories** to identify top performers.
    
*   **Customer Demographics**: Analyzes shopping behavior by gender, revealing that **female customers** account for **57.17%** of total transactions.
    
*   **Sales Trends**: Includes a time-series analysis highlighting daily revenue fluctuations between **January and March 2019**.
    

Tools Used
----------

*   **Power BI Desktop**: For data modeling, DAX calculations, and report visualization.
    
*   **Data Source**: Supermarket transactional dataset (CSV format).
    

How to Use the Dashboard
------------------------

1.  **Filters**: Use the slicers to filter data by Branch, Customer Type, or Date.
    
2.  **Interactivity**: Click on specific product categories in the bar charts to cross-filter the entire dashboard and see specific demographic or regional performance for that category.
    
3.  **KPI Cards**: Reference the top ribbon for real-time totals of the selected data range.
    

**Strategic Recommendations**
-----------------------------

*   **Increase Membership**: Convert "Normal" customers to "Members" by offering instant sign-up discounts to boost higher spending habits.
    
*   **Target Male Shoppers**: Launch specific marketing for men to increase their transaction share from the current **42.83%**.
    
*   **Promote Health & Beauty**: Use bundle deals to raise sales in this category, which currently has the lowest volume at **854 units**.
    
*   **Improve Ratings**: Investigate the **6.98 average rating** to identify and fix specific service or product quality issues.
    
*   **Scale Best Practices**: Apply successful strategies from the **Alex branch** to Giza and Cairo to balance profits.
